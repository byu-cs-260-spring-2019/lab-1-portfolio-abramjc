The Basics

My name is Abram Clements. I'm from Eagle Mountain, Utah, though I'd rather be from Oregon, because I think it's absolutely beautiful there! I love the beaches, forests, and mountains! One of my favorite things to do is to go hiking. I am a student at Brigham Young University, studying Computer Science. In high school and junior high I thought I was going to study Music. I was in my school band and choir as well as an extra-curricular symphony and choir. I took private lessons on brass and piano. I was really into music. I still am, but I don't perform or practice anywhere near as much as I used to. It was my Senior year in high school and on my mission when I felt like I was supposed to study something else, and after lots of consideration and thinking, I decided on computer science.

I come from a fairly big family. I am the oldest of seven kids, I have four younger sisters and two younger brothers (who were born on the same day 12 years apart btw).
